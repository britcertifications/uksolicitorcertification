Why us

Why use a UK-qualified solicitor in Dubai?

Many UK documents must be certified, attested or witnessed by a UK-qualified solicitor before they are accepted by HM Land Registry, UK banks, HMRC or the Office of the Public Guardian. A UAE notary, a MOFA stamp or a Hague apostille will not satisfy these requirements. If you are a British expat in Dubai, this normally means flying back to the UK or finding a UK qualified solicitor in the region. This is inconvenient and rarely cost effective.

We are a team of UK-qualified solicitors based in Dubai, regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority of England and Wales. We provide low cost certification, attestation and witnessing services to the standard your UK paperwork requires, with same-day turnaround in most cases.

Cash on collection. No retainer, no surprises.
